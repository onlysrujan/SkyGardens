New year Celebration

It is celebrated on January 1 yearly.The earliest recorded celebration of the new year is recorded back some 4,000 years to ancient Babylon. For Babylonians, the first full moon following the vernal equinox, the day in late March with full sunlight and darkness, was the start of the new year

Republic Day

Republic Day is a public holiday in India where the country marks and celebrates the date on which the Constitution of India came into effect on 26 January 1950. This replaced the Government of India Act 1935 as the governing document of India, thus turning the nation into a republic separate from British Raj.

Holi

The ritual starts by lighting up the bonfire one day before the day of Holi and this process symbolizes the triumph of good over the bad. On the day of Holi people play with colours with their friends and families and in evening they show love and respect to their close ones with Abeer.
